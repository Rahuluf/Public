About Us

Established in 1993 by visionary founder Mr. Ranjit Kr. Das, Lifeline Hospital began its journey with just 20 beds, driven by a mission to provide quality and affordable healthcare to the people of Silchar and surrounding regions. Over the years, through dedication and trust, it has grown into a premier 50-bedded hospital, recognized for its commitment to compassionate care and clinical excellence. With a patient-first philosophy and a team of experienced medical professionals, the hospital has become a trusted name in the local healthcare landscape.

Strategically located in Rangirkhari, one of the central areas of Silchar city, Lifeline Hospital is easily accessible via bus, shared auto, and other public transport, making it convenient for patients from both urban and rural areas. The hospital offers a broad range of medical services under one roof, ensuring timely and effective care without the burden of high costs. Over three decades, Lifeline Hospital has stayed true to its founding values—providing affordable, accessible, and quality healthcare to all sections of society.

Departments

Lifeline Hospital offers a wide range of medical and surgical specialties, designed to deliver comprehensive and coordinated care to patients of all ages. Each department is staffed by experienced specialists and supported by modern diagnostic and treatment facilities.

Department of Surgery

Our skilled team of surgeons, anesthetists, and surgical nurses specialize in a wide range of procedures including general surgery, laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery, trauma surgery, and minor to major elective operations.

Department of Dentistry

Our experienced dental professionals specialize in the diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of a wide range of dental and gum-related issues, including tooth decay, gum disease, dental infections, tooth extractions, and oral hygiene management.

Department of Dermatology

Our dermatologists are skilled in managing both common and complex issues such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, fungal infections, hair loss, skin allergies, nail disorders, pigmentation, scars, and premature aging.

Additional Services

Lifeline Hospital’s Skin Department offers a range of additional services to address both cosmetic and medical needs. These include braces for teeth alignment, hair transplant procedures to restore hair growth, and facial aesthetic treatments such as Botox and fillers for a refreshed appearance. The department also provides advanced laser treatments for skin rejuvenation, hair removal, and pigmentation issues. Expert care is available for head and neck trauma, managing facial injuries and related conditions. Additionally, the hospital offers various plastic surgeries, including both cosmetic and reconstructive procedures to enhance or restore appearance.

Head & Neck Trauma

Head & Neck Trauma care at Lifeline Hospital involves the expert management of injuries affecting the face, skull, jaw, and neck. Our specialized team is equipped to handle both minor and complex trauma cases, including fractures, soft tissue injuries, and internal damage resulting from accidents or physical impact.

Plastic Surgeries

Plastic Surgeries at Lifeline Hospital encompass a wide range of both cosmetic and reconstructive procedures aimed at enhancing appearance and restoring function. Our expert plastic surgeons perform treatments such as rhinoplasty (nose reshaping), liposuction, tummy tuck (abdominoplasty), scar revision, and burn reconstruction.
